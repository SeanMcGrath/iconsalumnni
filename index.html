We are ready to change the world.

iCAN is the professional and social network for graduates of iCons, a next-generation of program of interdisciplinary science and engineering education. Armed with four years of training in critical thinking, scientific problem solving, and communication, our members are ready to take on the world's biggest challenges.


Professional Development

iCAN members work in diverse industries, from biological research to clean energy to software development, and strive to be leaders in our fields. The primary function of iCAN is to foster the exchange of ideas, resources, and people between academic and industrial disciplines.

Social Networking

iCAN works to bring our members together outside of work as well.
