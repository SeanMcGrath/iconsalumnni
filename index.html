Volunteering

Giving back to the community is extremely important. As iCons Alum, we have a duty to spread the word of the benefits of integrated science. iCAN teams up with nonprofits and other organizations to give back to the community.

Academics

Where would we be without the future iCAN members - the current iCons undergrads! iCAN organizes events for current iCons students to help with their academics, careers, and professional development.

Networking

Expanding our reach is key, as is strengthening bonds between iCAN members. Networking includes anything from a small local get together of iCAN members to a larger-scale event with influential and important people.
